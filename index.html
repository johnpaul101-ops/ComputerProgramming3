<Who we are/>

We are CP3, a group of tech-driven students who believe that learning gets better when it's done together.Every day in our section feels like a mix of challenges, laughter, and those “wait, why isn't this code working?” moments.But that's what makes us who we are: curious, determined, and always ready to grow.Whether it's building projects, solving errors, or helping each other out, we're learning to code not just computers, but our own future too.
